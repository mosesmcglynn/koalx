background image:
dito gagamit tayo ng url to specify our image na gagamitin.

no-repeat:
The background-image is not repeated. The image will only be shown once	

repeat-x:
The background image is repeated only horizontally or in x axis

repeat-y:
The background image is repeated only vertically or in y axis

cover:
Resizes the background image to cover the entire container

contain:
Resizes the background image to make sure the image is fully visible	

length:
Sets the width and height of the background image
