Haircuts

Undercut

This style features shorter sides and back, with longer hair on top, providing a clean and sharp look.

Fade Haircut

A fade involves a gradual transition from short to longer hair, creating a polished and modern appearance.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic men’s hairstyle that is very popular in the military and in the sporting world, mainly due to its practicality and low maintenance.

Pompadour

The pompadour is a hairstyle named after Madame de Pompadour (1721–1764), a mistress of King Louis XV of France.

Quiff

The quiff is a hairstyle that combines the 1950s pompadour hairstyle, the 1950s flattop, and sometimes a mohawk.

Comb Over

A classic style where the hair is combed to one side, often with a part, creating a polished and timeless appearance.
